The Best Cafe & Diner in Bracknell

The Cafe

Located on the beautiful water side of Mill Pond, Bracknell Berkshire lies one of the best kept secrets in the county, home of the full English The Cafe at Millpond.

With stunning scenary, local wildlife and swans fequently spotted enjoying the idyllic location in the heart of Bracknell, you will find one of the finest breakfasts the area has to offer at a price to please everybody. Millpond cafe also has an extensive playground nextdoor for little ones to burn off all that extra energy.

Testimony

From Pierce Brosnan to a wooden pallet covered in fry-up classics, we were well amongst stellar company from the moment we entered. En route to examine some Uni accommodation, we felt hungry, weathered and cold. Over the horizon, the lakeside Cafe glow beaconed us in. I am pleased to write that our expectations were exceeded and we were in no way disappointed. One of my colleagues suffers a gluten intolerance, which is at the best of times an inconvenience. However, the delightful, warming and charming owner went above and beyond to accommodate our every need… a sweet cannoli replaced a coffee biscuit, a tub of hummus and a nugget of Falafel replaced the lack of gluten free bread. This was more than satisfactory and a true display of the extraordinary customer service. Summing up a this experience can be done in three words: Splendour Pride Creation. In short, it was nice.

“ I would recommend anybody with kids to take the time to enjoy life here, cheap prices great food and a park for the kids on the lakeside, wonderful. ”

— Jane Smith
